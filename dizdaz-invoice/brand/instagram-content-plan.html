Posting Tips for Jodie

📸 Photography

Natural light is best. Film before and after at the same angle. Wide shots for rooms, close-ups for detail. Landscape for Reels, portrait (4:5) for feed posts.

🕐 Best Posting Times

Mondays 9–11am, Wednesdays 12–2pm, Fridays 8–10am, Sundays 7–9pm. Post when your target audience (property managers) is checking their phone.

💬 Reply to Every Comment

In the first hour after posting, reply to every comment. Instagram rewards engagement. Even "Thanks 🙏" is enough. Ask a follow-up question to keep the thread going.

📱 Stories Daily

Even on non-posting days, add a Story. Behind the scenes, a quick room shot, a poll ("Do you manage short-lets in Bristol?"). Stories keep you visible between posts.

🔗 Link in Bio

Keep the link in bio pointing to the website (www.dizdazcleaning.com). Mention it in captions once or twice a week to drive traffic.

✅ Sign-Off Reminder

Any post mentioning YourApartment by name, quoting a client, or making a specific claim needs Jodie's sign-off before publishing. When in doubt, check with CB first.
