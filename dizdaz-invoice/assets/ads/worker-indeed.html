About Us
DizDaz Cleaning Company is a professional cleaning service based in Bristol, specialising in Airbnb and serviced apartment turnovers. We work with leading short-let operators across the city and are known for our reliability, attention to detail, and friendly team culture.
The Role
You will be responsible for preparing apartments to a high standard between guest stays. This includes cleaning, linen changes, restocking amenities, and reporting any maintenance issues. No two days are exactly the same, and you'll be part of a supportive, professional team.
